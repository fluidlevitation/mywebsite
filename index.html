Welcome to our English-speaking scuba diving service in Okinawa, Japan, catering for those wishing to scuba dive at Cape Manza (Onna), the Kerama Islands and beyond! We offer Fun Diving Trips for certified scuba divers to various dive sites around Okinawa, a wide range of PADI Scuba Diving Courses for those who want to pick up diving or improve their skills, Discover Scuba Diving or Snorkeling for guests without a license, and Boat Charters.

Our Fun Diving Trips thoughout Okinawa are really diverse and the crystal clear visibility under water is second to none! Let us take you to the stunning Manza Dream Hole or the popular Maeda Blue Cave near Onna village, the spectacular reefs of the Kerama Islands, or the historic USS Emmons wreck from WWII. We can also visit other locations such as Minna Island, Itoman, Cape Hedo, Sunabe, and more. Please check out our fun diving trip itineraries.

You can also choose among our many Advanced and Specialty PADI Scuba Diving Courses if you are already certified, but are seeking to develop your skills further. Please check out our course offerings.

If you are new to scuba diving and would like to get an Open Water Scuba Certification, give Discover Scuba Diving a try, or jump in the ocean for a bit of Snorkeling, you have come to the right place! Please check out our offers for beginners.

Our Okinawa scuba diving operation is all about providing a safe and personalized experience under water. We will make sure that you leave the island with memories, which will last a lifetime.

If you are a CERTIFIED diver

Fun Diving Trips in Okinawa

Join the fun diving trips we organize all over Okinawa! We keep our groups small, so you will get plenty of individual attention from the guide.

Depending on the location, 2 dives cost between ¥ 14000 and ¥ 18000, while 3 dives are ¥ 18000 - ¥ 21000. Single, night dives, and shore dives are also available.

Please choose a scuba diving area from the selection below or visit the complete list of all diving locations in Okinawa we operate at.

MANZA

Cape Manza, Onna - scuba diving for all levels around the famous Manza Dream Hole

KERAMAS

Kerama Islands - exciting all-day trips in a wonderful and peaceful ocean paradise

EMMONS

USS Emmons - deep and mysterious WWII wreck for the experienced

MINNA

Minna Island - supreme relaxation amid vivid reefs near Churaumi Aquarium

ITOMAN

Itoman - pristine conditions a stone's throw away from Naha Airport

HEDO

Cape Hedo - drift divers' utopia and home to the Hedo Dome cave

Advanced and Specialty Diving Courses

Improve your diving skills while in Okinawa with our range of advanced and specialty PADI scuba diving courses. For our training dives we visit sites all over Okinawa depending on the course requirements and your own preference.

Our courses are flexible and conducted in English. Training is mostly individual and the schedule can be adjusted to fit your schedule.

Please choose a scuba diving course from the selection below or visit the complete list of all our PADI course offerings.

ADVANCED

Advanced Open Water - take your diving skills beyond the foundational level

RESCUE

Rescue - acquire the knowledge to handle and prevent diving emergencies

DEEP

Deep Diver - gain the skills to safely venture deeper into the ocean abyss

NITROX

Nitrox - extend your bottom time by learning how to use enriched air mixtures

SOLO

Self-Reliant Diver - step up your game to a whole new level of diving

If you are NEW to scuba diving

Become scuba certified while in Okinawa with our Open Water Diver Course! We promise it will be an experience that you will remember for the years to come.

Alternatively, you can sign up for our Discover Scuba Diving program or our Snorkeling trips! Either of those will give you a glimpse of the underwater world and will leave you hungry for more.

For all three of the activities - Open Water Diver Course, Discover Scuba diving, and Snorkeling - we offer both beach and boat entry options. Beach entry is available at Maeda Blue Cave, Sunabe (Chatan), and Motobu, while boat entry is available at Cape Manza, the Kerama Islands, and Minna Island.
