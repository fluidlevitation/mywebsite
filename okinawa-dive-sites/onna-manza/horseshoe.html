Horseshoe

Diving area

Onna Manza

Experience level

Intermediate
Advanced

Max. depth

40 m

Access

Shore
Boat

Possibility to see

sharks, nudibranch

Horseshoe combines very interesting rock formations with the possibility to spot larger marine life. It consists of a vertical drop from the reef, a large rock at a depth of 30+ meters, a giant crack in the wall, as well as two swim-throughs that spiral up from the deep. The wall is home to many octopuses and nudibranches. Large schools of fish often times swim by and the corals at shallower depth are splendid.
