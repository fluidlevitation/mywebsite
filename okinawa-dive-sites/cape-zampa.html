Cape Zampa

Cape Zampa offers one of the best advanced diving in Okinawa. Most interesting topographical features are located at depths below 20 meters, all the way down to 40 meters, and the currents can be quite strong. As a result, this area is accessible only to divers with sufficient skills and experience in deep diving. If you are one of those people, you will certainly enjoy the numerous swim-throughs, caverns and pinnacles under water. In addition, since this area is quite exposed, it is often home to large fish such as tuna and giant trevally. When the boat to the Kerama Islands cannot make it there due to unfavorable conditions, Cape Zampa is one of the places we visit instead.

Details

Appropriate for: advanced, deep and technical divers

Minimum number of participants: 2
Minimum number of dives per participant: 2

Boats leave daily at 8:30am and 1:30pm and each outing includes two dives.

Departure is from the port near Cape Maeda in Onna village. We offer free pick-up service from hotels in Onna, Yomitan, and Chatan. Pick-ups from other locations are also possible upon request. Please inquire.

Boat travel time to the dive sites is ~10 min.

Toilet available at the port.
