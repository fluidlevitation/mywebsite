Cape Manza (Onna Village)

Since we are located in central Okinawa, the dive sites around Cape Manza near Onna village are literally in our backyard, which is why we can visit them at any time on a daily basis. Luckily for our guests (and for us), these dive sites are among the most beautiful on Okinawa main island. The terrain that they offer is extremely varied – from sandy bottom with splendid coral mounds, to vertical walls that descend into the abyss as well as a number of caverns and swim-throughs. The marine life that can be spotted here is equally diverse – from large animals, such as turtles, rays and sharks, to smaller critters like nudibranchs, shrimp and crabs.

Details

Appropriate for: beginners to advanced divers and snorkelers

Minimum number of participants: 1
Minimum number of dives per participant: 1

Boats leave daily at 9am, 11am, 1:30pm, and (occasionally) 3:30pm and return to port after each dive.

Departure is from the port near Hyatt Regency Hotel in Onna. We offer free pick-up service from hotels in Onna, Yomitan and Chatan. Pick-ups from other locations are also possible upon request. Please inquire.

Boat travel time to the dive sites is from ~5 min to ~15 min, which makes it appropriate for guests prone to sea sickness.

Toilet and rinsing water available at the port.

Shore diving is also available for some of the dive sites at any time except during adverse sea conditions. Please note that it is necessary to walk about 150 m on top of sharp rocks in order to get from the parking lot to the water.

We conduct many of our scuba diving courses in this dive area.

Please note: Occasionally guests request diving a particular dive site (e.g. Mini Dream Hole). Although we always do our best, we cannot promise to consistently satisfy such demands if you do only 1 dive with us on a given day. This is due to the fact that we share the boat with other dive shops whose customers might have different requests. If, however, you dive with us 3 or 4 times in a day, we can guarantee that at least one of the dives will be at a dive site of your choice (if the sea conditions allow it). Very often, though, our guests are lucky and we manage to dive all of their top choices!
