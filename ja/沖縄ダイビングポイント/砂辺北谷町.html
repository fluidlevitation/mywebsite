砂辺（北谷町）

The Sunabe seawall area in Chatan is a promenade that stretches for about 5 km along the ocean shore just north of the American village with a number of staircases leading into the water. Unlike other places in Okinawa, the corals here are soft which makes this area special. Since the entry points are literally just off the street, Sunabe is a favorite spot for night diving as well. Beneath the water it is customary to see barracudas, nudibranchs as well as a variety of critters. Upon being done with diving for the day, guests are encouraged to try the famous Blue Seal ice-cream with traditional Okinawan flavors from the nearby shop.

Please scroll down for details and prices. Towards the bottom of this page you can also find detailed descriptions of individual dive sites located around Sunabe, such as the Water Treatment Plant, Curry House, or North Steps.
