General remarks:

All prices above are per person and are listed in Japanese Yen (¥). 10% Japanese sales tax is included. Lunch is not included but can be ordered/purchased separately.

All dives are on air with the exception for the Enriched Air course. It is possible to dive nitrox, however that would cost ¥ 800 more per tank.

The amounts shown are valid when paying cash. Payment using a credit card via PayPal is also possible, however in that case an additional 4.5% service fee is incurred.
